SRL : Dependency Injection Framework

Compile time safe validation of DI

Swift and Objective-C cross-compatible

Extremely fast injection and validation

Adopted by >90% of top-30 Google apps

iRPC : Standard RPC Framework

Standardizes across multiple legacy & modern RPC stacks at Google

Future proof to new RPC systems

Adopted by >60% of top-30 Google apps

Heimdall : Conformance Framework

Enables apps to define and enforce code structure, dependency or architectural rules, at compile time

Offers powerful tooling for managing violations and exemptions

Adopted by Maps, YouTube, Gmail, Search, Chrome, Home...
